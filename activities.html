Activities at Mamacha Gaon Resort

Mamacha gaon resort has various activities where everyone can enjoy whole day and experience enjoyment of real ethnic rural life at resort. Mamacha Gaon is fully equipped with swimming pool, indoor games, bullock cart ride, rain dance & facility like trekking on a nearby mountain.
