Amenities at Mamacha Gaon Resort

You can find modern amenities combined with exotic natural beauty at our place. The cottages, equipped with latest amenities, are nestled in mountains covered by lush greens and topped by fresh blues where you can relax, unwind or simply take a round.

Swimming Pool

The pool has a feel-good ambiance with lush greenery and quiet, perfect for a family or a group experience. Get a quick dip in this pool on your weekends.

Rain Dance

For unlimited fun or entertainment Mamacha Gaon is an ideal place for you. We have rain dance area where you can enjoy with your loved once.

DJ

We have DJ at resort which make you groove on music with your loved ones.
