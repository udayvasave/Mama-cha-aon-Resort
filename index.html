How to Reach

Mamacha Gaon Resort is located at Gundale village, Boisar East. It is easily accessible from nearby cities like Mumbai, Thane, Vapi and Surat. Resort is located at just 11kms away from Mumbai-Ahmedabad National highway 8. Taxi service is available from Palghar and Boisar stations.
Nearest Station:
Boisar - 10 kms, Palghar - 21 kms. Feel free to contact us anytime if you have any trouble to locate us.

To make your experience as rich and beautiful as possible, our dedicated team is at your service. So for any queries, special requests or to book your stay at Mama cha Gaon Resort, please get in touch with us.
